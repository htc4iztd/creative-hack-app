announcement Announcements

Welcome to the Creative.hack Platform! This platform is designed to facilitate KDDI's in-house ideathon and technical contest.

The first half of the year is dedicated to the Service Development Contest (Ideathon), where you can submit business plans and vote for the best ideas.

The second half focuses on the Technical Contest, where you can implement proof-of-concept (PoC) projects based on selected business plans or submit purely technical ideas.

description Business Plans

Submit your business ideas, view other submissions, and vote for the best plans. Selected plans will move forward to the PoC phase.

code PoC Plans

Browse technical projects, join existing teams, or create your own technical proposal. Showcase your technical skills and innovation.
